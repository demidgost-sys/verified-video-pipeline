# Code of conduct

## Our standard

We want a focused, respectful, and technically honest project. Participants are
expected to discuss ideas and evidence without harassment, personal attacks,
discrimination, intimidation, or publication of another person's private data.

Constructive disagreement is welcome. Critique the contract, implementation,
test, or claim—not the person. Be explicit about uncertainty and do not inflate
a synthetic proof into a production claim.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, commits, issues, and
other contributions that violate this standard. Repeated or severe violations
may result in a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository owner's GitHub profile
or an appropriate private GitHub reporting channel. Do not include another
person's sensitive information in a public report.

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone is publicly representing
the project.
